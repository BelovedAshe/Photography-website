THE PERSON BEHIND THE CAMERA

Photography has been part of my life since I got my first camera at 18, and over the past 7 years, it's grown into a passion that's all about capturing life as it unfolds-candid, real, and authentic moments over stiff, posed shots. I officially stepped into the business side of photography in 2021 but took a creative & maternal break from 2023-2025, I'm excited to be back with a renewed perspective and inspiration. What drinks me is the joy of capturing those in-between moments-the fleeting glances, bursts of laughter, and stories that pictures can tell without a single word. If you're looking for a photographer who values authenticity and creativity, I'd love to hear from you.

Let's create something beautiful together.
